# Quiz Landing Page Video Script (v3 Final)

## Teleprompter Version

Every business owner I talk to is losing time in the same five places. Most of them don't even know which ones.

I'm James. I run Beyond Seven Studios.

A year ago, half the stuff I needed to do in my business, I just wasn't doing. Not because I was lazy. Because there weren't enough hours.

Newsletter, lead gen, outreach, content, support. All manual. All me. Spread across tools that didn't talk to each other. No system. Just chaos.

Then I started building everything through one system. Things I wasn't doing at all started getting done. My newsletter research went from something I'd skip most weeks to 10 minutes.

Right now there's a gap opening up between businesses that have AI systems running and businesses that are still doing everything by hand. And that gap is getting wider every week.

So I built a quiz. 10 questions about how you run your business right now. It analyses your answers and gives you a personalised breakdown of where you're leaking time, what to fix first, and whether to build it yourself or get someone to do it for you. Nobody else gets the same result.
