## CREATIVE DIRECTION

**Recommended primary format: Raw Native (Sabri's #1)**

Phone photo of James at his desk looking directly at camera, slightly frustrated expression, laptop open with too many tabs visible. Should look like a friend posted it, not a brand. No polished graphics. No text overlay on the image itself.

**Secondary test: Native Social Post**

Split image. Left side: a messy desk with sticky notes, multiple screens, scattered notebooks (the "wearing every hat" visual). Right side: clean desk, single screen, coffee. The before/after contrast stops the scroll and the dual-image technique forces the brain to process both simultaneously.

**Third test: SMS Screenshot**

Fake text conversation:
- "mate how do you get everything done"
- "I stopped doing everything"
- "what do you mean"
- "took a quiz. found out where I was wasting 15 hours a week. fixed 3 things. that's it"

This format looks native to the feed and reads like content, not an ad.
